How it works

Each algorithm targets a specific market condition. Together they form an uncorrelated portfolio designed to perform across varying regimes.

01

Multi-Strategy Portfolio

Independent algorithms covering trend-following, mean reversion, breakout, and momentum — each specialising in different market conditions and sessions.

02

Regime Detection

Real-time market regime classification automatically enables and disables strategies based on current conditions. Trend algorithms don't run in ranging markets.

03

Autonomous Risk Control

Three independent safety layers: per-algorithm circuit breakers, system-wide equity monitoring, and daily human oversight. The system protects capital autonomously.

04

Verified Performance

All results tracked and independently verified through third-party platforms. No black boxes. Monte Carlo simulation validates forward-looking probability.

Philosophy

Operating principles

  • 01

    Automation over intuition

    Every trading decision is made by code, not emotion. Human oversight monitors the system — it doesn't override it.

  • 02

    Capital preservation first

    Risk management is the primary function. Circuit breakers, position limits, and equity monitoring all prioritise protecting capital over capturing returns.

  • 03

    Evidence-based decisions

    No strategy runs live without statistical validation. Monte Carlo simulation, walk-forward testing, and minimum sample sizes gate every deployment.

  • 04

    Simple beats complex

    Robust systems use proven techniques. No black-box ML, no overfit parameters, no complexity for its own sake. Transparent logic that can be audited and explained.
