How it works

The approach

A team of specialist AI agents — drawn from multiple vendors, each deployed to its relative strength — research strategies, govern risk, write code and run operations. Deterministic systems execute. Three independent safety layers protect capital at all times. One human director retains absolute authority at every level.

Risk philosophy

Constraint before return

Capital protection is the primary function. Every rule below is system-enforced and cannot be overridden during live deployment.

What we do
  • Hard position limits — maximum risk per trade defined and enforced automatically
  • Tiered circuit breakers — three independent layers each capable of halting trading autonomously
  • News filters — trading pauses automatically around high-impact events
  • Statistical gating — no strategy goes live without passing a rigorous evidence threshold
  • Regime awareness — strategies only activate in the conditions they were built for
What we never do
  • No martingale — position size never increases after a loss
  • No grid trading — no averaging down on open positions
  • No recovery mode — losses are accepted, never chased
  • No black boxes — every strategy has explicit, auditable logic
  • No parameter changes under pressure — rules defined in calm, enforced in volatility

Strategy runs deep

It started with writing computer games in BASIC as a child, then chess at junior national level. Both taught the same lesson: understand the system completely before you try to beat it — pattern recognition, consequence thinking, the discipline to play the position rather than the emotion.

In 2024 he began building the systems that became IPQI, applying the same programme-management frameworks to a new domain. The instruments are different.
