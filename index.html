How it works

The architecture

A structured multi-agent workflow — research, risk governance, code delivery, decision authority — with one director reviewing and approving every action. No single agent can approve and execute its own output. No human writes trading code directly.

Risk philosophy

Capital protection first

Every constraint is system-enforced and cannot be overridden in live deployment.

What we do
  • Hard position limits — maximum risk per trade enforced automatically
  • Tiered circuit breakers — three independent layers, each capable of halting trading
  • News blackouts — trading pauses automatically around high-impact events
  • Statistical gating — no strategy goes live without passing a rigorous evidence threshold
  • Regime awareness — strategies activate only in the conditions they were designed for
What we never do
  • No martingale — position size never increases after a loss
  • No grid trading — no averaging down on open positions
  • No recovery mode — losses are accepted, never chased
  • No opaque systems — no capital in strategies that cannot be understood, tested and governed
  • No pressure overrides — parameters defined in calm, enforced in volatility
