How it works

The approach

AI researches and builds. Deterministic systems trade. Independent safety layers protect capital. A human director retains final authority over all strategic, risk, and deployment decisions.

Risk philosophy

Professionals talk risk first

IPQI deploys proprietary capital and participates in institutional prop firm programmes. In both cases, the same rules apply: capital protection is the primary function, not an afterthought.

What we do
  • Hard position limits — maximum risk per trade defined and enforced automatically
  • Tiered circuit breakers — three independent layers each capable of halting trading autonomously
  • News filters — trading pauses automatically around high-impact events
  • Regime awareness — strategies only activate in the conditions they were built for
  • Statistical gating — no strategy goes live without passing a rigorous evidence threshold first
What we never do
  • No martingale — we never increase size after a losing trade
  • No grid trading — no compounding of open positions to average down
  • No recovery mode — losses are accepted, never chased
  • No black boxes — every strategy has explicit, auditable logic
  • No rules changed under pressure — decisions made in calm are enforced in volatile conditions

Origin

The same principles, a different game

It started earlier than the career. Writing computer games in BASIC as a child, then chess at national level — both taught the same lesson: understand the system completely before you try to beat it. Pattern recognition. Consequence thinking. The discipline to play the position, not the emotion.

That ran as an undercurrent through 25 years managing high-stakes technology programmes into tier one financial institutions — how to hold a framework under pressure, how to manage risk when consequences are eight figures, how to make decisions under uncertainty and live with them.

In 2024 Ian began building the trading systems that became IPQI, applying the same programme-management frameworks to a new domain. The instruments are different. The timescales are shorter. The fundamentals — process over intuition, evidence over conviction, rules defined in calm and followed under pressure — are identical.

  • 01

    Automation over intuition

    Every trade is backed by statistical validation across hundreds of data points. Analysis at a depth and speed no human could replicate in real time.

  • 02

    Capital preservation first

    Risk management is the primary function of the entire system. Multiple independent safety layers all prioritise protecting capital over capturing returns.

  • 03

    Evidence before deployment

    No strategy runs live without statistical validation across a meaningful sample. The bar is high by design — promising early results are not enough.

  • 04

    Simple beats complex

    Robust systems use proven techniques applied rigorously. No over-optimised parameters, no complexity for its own sake.
